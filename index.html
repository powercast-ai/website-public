Our products

We currently offer 3 forecast outputs for Great Britain (GB) as standard; demand, embedded solar generation and embedded wind generation.

All forecasts express an average MW value for each settlement period and are generated at national level for the GB market.

The time horizon covers the short-term trading cycle, optimized for trading in the intraday and day-ahead trading windows.

The forecast outputs are made available through an API endpoint and can be retrieved in your preferred data format, such as csv or json.

Demand

The national demand forecast includes transmission losses, but excludes interconnector flows and demand from station transformers and pumped storage units. As such, it is a standardized data point which can be directly interpreted and compared to standardized industry and public data, such as the Initial National Demand Out-turn (INDO) and National Demand Forecast (NDF) from the National Energy System Operator (NESO).

Embedded Solar

National embedded solar generation is also considered "invisible" as it reflects generation from solar pv panels, whether domestic or commercial, connected to the distribution network. Both individual embedded generation forecasts are used as data input for the national demand forecast.

Embedded Wind

The national embedded wind generation forecast estimates electricity output generated from wind turbines connected to the distribution grid. This generation does not have transmission system metering installed and is therefore often considered "invisible". The effect of embedded generation is a suppression of demand at the level of the transmission grid.
